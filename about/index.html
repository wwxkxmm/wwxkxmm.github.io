About

Xumeng Wang received her B.S. in Information and Computing Science from the Zhejiang University, China in 2016. She was a joint Ph.D. student at University of California, Davis. She is currently a PhD candidate in the College of Computer Science and Technology at the Zhejiang University. Her research interests include visual analysis, privacy preservation and urban data.
